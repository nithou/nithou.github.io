Zweig is a free minimalist WordPress Theme, created to support a comfortable reading and writing experience. The aim of Zweig is to focus on the things you write, not on the usual "side-dishes" that invade the usual blogs.

It features a clean and minimalist one column design, a three widget area footer, a single-level menu header and full ligatures support. It's build on HTML5 and CSS3 and fully responsive.

You can preview it and download it as you want!
